### How to use

1. Select your Robot/NPC in Unity.
2. Find the **Puter Speaker** script.
3. Copy and Paste the **Provider**, **Model**, and **Voice id** exactly as shown above.
4. (Optional) If ElevenLabs is slow, you can switch Provider to `openai` and Voice id to `alloy` for a fast, free alternative.
